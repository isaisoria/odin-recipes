Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a Italian pasta dish. The dish is comprised of layers of large pasta noodles with meat and tomato-based sauce inbetween each of the layers of pasta.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Cooking Directions:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
